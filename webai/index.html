🔍 What is AI? Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to mimic human intelligence.
It enables computers to learn from data, solve problems, and make decisions.
💡 Key Features of AI Machine Learning – AI learns from experience (data) without being explicitly programmed.
Natural Language Processing – AI understands and generates human language (like Siri or ChatGPT).
Computer Vision – AI sees and understands images and videos.
Autonomous Systems – Self-driving cars and drones use AI to operate without human control.
🧠 Benefits of AI Faster decision-making with high accuracy.
Automation of repetitive tasks in industries, customer service, and more.
Personalized experiences in apps, games, ads, and learning platforms.
24/7 availability – AI bots don’t need sleep.
