⚠️ Negatives of AI Job Loss

Automation by AI can replace human jobs, especially in sectors like manufacturing, customer support, and transportation.
Lack of Human Judgment

AI lacks empathy, creativity, and moral values — it can make decisions without understanding emotions or ethics.

High Cost of Development

Creating, training, and maintaining AI systems requires a lot of money, time, and expertise.

Privacy Issues

AI systems often collect and analyze personal data, raising serious concerns about surveillance and privacy.
